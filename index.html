About

I am currently employed as a full-time Ph.D. student in the 7th semester at DMIS, where I have the privilege of working under the guidance of Professor Jaewoo Kang at Korea University. Prior to this, I obtained my BEng degree from Vietnam National University, Hanoi. I also successfully completed my M.S. in Computer Science and Engineering at Gachon University, under the supervision of Professor Sangwoong Lee.

The majority of my individual research involves the integration of data science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and formal reasoning in order to accelerate bio/medical science in the service of people.

In particular, below are some keywords of my recent interests:

  • Multimodal learning
  • 3D geometric information
  • Equivariant neural network
  • Drug discovery (Drug target interaction)
